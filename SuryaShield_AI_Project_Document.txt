SURYA SHIELD AI Intelligent Space Weather Forecasting and Impact
Assessment Platform

1.  CORE VISION

SuryaShield AI is an AI-powered space weather early warning system built
using ISRO Aditya-L1 mission data. It combines Soft X-ray observations
from SoLEXS and Hard X-ray observations from HEL1OS to detect ongoing
solar flares and predict future flares before they cause disruptions on
Earth.

Our goal is to transform raw solar X-ray signals into intelligent alerts
that can protect satellite communication, GPS navigation, radio systems,
power infrastructure, and future space missions.

2.  PROBLEM WE ARE SOLVING

Solar flares are sudden explosions of energy from the Sun. Strong flares
can affect modern technology on Earth and in space.

Current detection systems often identify flares after they have already
started. Our system focuses on two major tasks:

-   Nowcasting: Detecting and classifying solar flares in real time.
-   Forecasting: Predicting the probability of a flare occurring in the
    next few minutes.

The challenge is identifying hidden precursor patterns in X-ray data
before the flare reaches its peak.

3.  WHAT MAKES SURYASHIELD AI DIFFERENT

Most approaches use a single source of data or simple machine learning
models.

Our innovation: - Multi-modal fusion of SoLEXS and HEL1OS X-ray
signals. - Advanced time-series AI models such as CNN-LSTM and
Transformer-based architectures. - Real-time monitoring dashboard. -
Intelligent risk assessment system. - Visual alerts with confidence
scores and estimated lead time.
